Hyperlumines

  • Respondere

At the moment my world disappeared, I felt fantastic, as if nothing else than my own perception and joy matters.

When my life was about to end, I found myself walking alone, opened eyes, on a lonely dusty road.

Joy and fear were my own engine, and my creation was only justified by the harm I could cause to others.

For my last day on earth, I cleaned up my entiere lifeplace just to show everyone how corrupted they all were, and it was wonderfull.

As if the clean up wasnt enough, I trapped my own shape of perception in this box, not for my own pleasure, but only to figure out how to do the same to anyone.

The perfect, the most divine I ever achieved, I created perfection with my bare hands, now blessed with the power of a god, the real power of creation in the palm of my own hands.

And with that luggage, I used the stupidity of the foolish to perform my own peculiar individuals circle. I created myself an entiere place where all these fools hanged on every single of my words.

In the theater of mind, my disciples were able to throw every propriety at my will, as a superior being, able to control people and to shape them as my own private soldiers.

At this point, I felt powerfull enough to create my own new world, not only as a perception, but as a complete reality.

But only negativity and fear were inspiring me, my very own muses, but I guess a wonderfull world cant be created over hate.
